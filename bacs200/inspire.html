Rosa Parks

"A hero is a person who is admired or idealized for courage, outstanding acheivements, or nobel qualities."

One particular hero that I admire is Rosa Parks. I admire the following characteristics she has.

  • Exceptionally Brave
  • Caring
  • Determined
  • Passionate

Rosa Parks is a civil rights activist who refused to give up her seat on a public bus to a white man. This action was followed by an arrest which sparked the civil rights movement in 1955.
