UNCO Monfort College of Business

The Monfort College of Business is one of the top business schools that helps students prepare for future careers. There Mission statement is exactly that... "The Monfort College of Business will serve as a leading provider of immersive business education and career prepartion".

This school helps its students accomplish this mission by equipping them with the necessary knowledge, tools, and inspiration to help them reach their potential. They promote entrepreneurship and professional engagement within the community as well as advancing business practice through quality research.
